Landfill e-Forms

Project Lead

The City of Los Angeles, Department of Sanitation had a long history of performing paper audits for their various landfill locations. They had to manually monitor methane emissions across each location and then manually enter the data into a computer after a long day.

As the Project Lead for the Landfill e-Forms Application project, we converted the Department of Sanitation's auditing system to completely electronic. We created an Android Application for the outdoor audit and a desktop application for the transfer of data.

The Android Application helped improve efficiency out in the field and reduce human error, as paper can often get damaged or lost altogether. After a long day of work, the field technician would head to their workstation and simply plug their device into a desktop and have all the data transfer over.

The Android Application was created using Android Studio and the web application was created with Angular 4, Spring, Hibernate, and Microsoft SQL Server for data storage.
